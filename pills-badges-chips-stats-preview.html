Pill / Badge / Chip / Stat — Proposed System Preview

This is a live mockup of the new rules, not yet applied to the real app: canonical pill radius 20px with no glow, badge radius unified to 4px, chips split into two sub-styles, stats de-boxed into plain text. Everything below uses real CSS states — hover your mouse over any element to see its actual hover behavior.

Nothing in the live app has been changed yet. This file only exists to preview the new look before you confirm it.

Pill
Both flavors share the shape (20px, 1.5px border, no glow). Selector pills stay colorless until chosen (matches how the Add/Edit modal already behaves); filter pills stay tinted with their color at all times and just dim when not selected, plus always show a live count — this lets you scan the whole palette in the sidebar at a glance. Both appear only on the panel background (modal / sidebar); other backgrounds shown below for reference only, since pills don't naturally sit there today.

Chip
Two sub-flavors sharing the chip grammar (99px, 1px border, own action): the filter chip shows an applied filter as "Label: Value" with its own × to remove it; the tag chip shows one raw value from a multi-value field with its own × to remove it. The sync chip (reclassified from "pill") is informational and only becomes clickable in its error/offline state, to trigger a retry.

Stat
No box at all — plain inline text. Not interactive, not per-item; an aggregate page-level readout. This is what most clearly separates it from the other three, which are all "a labeled box" of some kind.
