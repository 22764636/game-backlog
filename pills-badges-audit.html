App backgrounds used below

The app uses two backgrounds: the page background (--base) behind everything, and a slightly lighter panel background (--s1 / --cbg, same hex) used for the header, sidebar, modals, and game cards. Pills/badges that live on a card or in the sidebar are shown against the panel background below; anything shown loose is against the page background.

Card prices — are they badges?

Mixed answer. The regular price shown on every card (.cprice) is plain colored text with no background/border/padding — so structurally it is not a badge. But the gg.deals price overlay shown on wishlist cards (retail price, key-shop price, and the "near/at all-time-low" flags) are real badges: small rounded chips with a background, sitting on top of the cover art. Below is exactly how each looks, over a cover-art mock so the translucent backgrounds render correctly.
